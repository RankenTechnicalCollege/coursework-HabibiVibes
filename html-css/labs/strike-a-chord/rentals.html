Instrument Rentals

At Strike a Chord, we understand that investing in a musical instrument can be a big decision. That's why we offer a variety of rental options to help you get started on your musical journey without the upfront cost.

Available Instruments for Rent:

  • Guitars (Acoustic and Electric)
  • Pianos and Keyboards
  • Drum Sets
  • Violins
  • Flutes and Clarinets

Rental Terms:

Our rental agreements are flexible, allowing you to rent an instrument for as short as one month or as long as a year. We also offer discounts for long-term rentals and students enrolled in our lessons.

How to Rent:

To rent an instrument, simply visit our studio or contact us via email at lessons@strikeachord.net
