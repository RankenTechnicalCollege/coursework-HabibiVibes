Our Lessons

At Strike a Chord, we offer a variety of music lessons tailored to suit all skill levels and musical interests. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced musician aiming to refine your skills, we have something for everyone.

Guitar Lessons

Our guitar lessons cover everything from basic chords and strumming patterns to advanced techniques like fingerpicking and soloing. We offer lessons for acoustic, electric, and bass guitar.

Piano Lessons

Learn to play the piano with our comprehensive lessons that include music theory, sight-reading, and various playing styles. Suitable for all ages and skill levels.

Drum Lessons

Our drum lessons focus on rhythm, timing, and coordination. We teach various drumming styles including rock, jazz, and funk.

Vocal Lessons

Improve your singing technique with our vocal lessons that cover breath control, pitch, tone, and performance skills. Suitable for all genres of music.

Violin Lessons

Our violin lessons are designed for beginners to advanced players, focusing on technique, music reading, and performance skills. We cover classical, folk, and contemporary styles.

Special Workshops

We regularly host special workshops on topics such as songwriting, music production, and performance techniques. Check our website for upcoming events.
