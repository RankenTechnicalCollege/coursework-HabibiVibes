Personal Background

My golf journey began during 2021 out of curiosity. I never played in highschool, although now I wish I had.. I remember watching others on the course, fascinated by the mix
of skill and patience the game demanded. What began as casual interest quickly turned into something much bigger. Progression. Making contact was first, but then I began to draw
to the feeling of progression, and improving my swing, learning the strategy behind each shot, and appreciating the mental side of the game.

Over time, golf became more than just a sport to me-it became a way to spend time outdoors, connect with others, and push myself to grow. What started as a hobby has turned into
a lifelong passion, and each round continues to teach me something new.

Keep chasing that perfect round!
